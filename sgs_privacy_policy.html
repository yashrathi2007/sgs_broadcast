Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

SGS Silk Mills Pvt. Ltd. ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our retailers, wholesalers, and business partners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with us through our retailer registration form, WhatsApp communications, and related business tools.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from our retailers and wholesalers:

  • Business name and owner/contact name
  • Address, city, district, state, and pin code
  • Mobile number and email address
  • Date of birth and marriage anniversary (for relationship management)
  • Shop size and business details
  • Feedback and suggestions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To maintain our retailer and wholesaler database
  • To send business communications, promotional messages, and event invitations via WhatsApp
  • To wish you on birthdays and anniversaries
  • To improve our products and services based on your feedback
  • To invite you to SGS conferences and events
  • To manage our business relationships effectively

3. WhatsApp Communications

We use the WhatsApp Business API to send you template messages including promotional content, event invitations, and business updates. By providing your mobile number to SGS Silk Mills, you consent to receive such communications. You may opt out at any time by contacting us directly.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in our database hosted on Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only used internally by SGS Silk Mills Pvt. Ltd. for the purposes described in this policy. We may share data with service providers (such as cloud hosting and messaging platforms) solely to operate our business tools.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as you maintain a business relationship with SGS Silk Mills, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications

8. Cookies

Our retailer registration form and admin tools do not use tracking cookies. Any data stored in your browser (such as login sessions) is used solely for functionality and is not shared with third parties.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
